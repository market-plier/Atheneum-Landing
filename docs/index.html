Introduction to Atheneum

Atheneum is a marketplace-based platform that lets YouTubers easily sell custom or automatically-generated NFTs related to their videos. At the same time, the fans can simply purchase the NFTs to support the creator or to access additional content.

Monetizing videos with NFTs

YouTubers often struggle to make income with their content, especially if they get demonetized. Atheneum lets the creators have a consistent revenue based on NFT technology.
